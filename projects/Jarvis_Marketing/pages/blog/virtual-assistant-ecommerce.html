What to hand over on day one

The most effective e-commerce VA handoffs start with one inbox and one set of rules. Before your VA's first day, you define three things:

1. Standard issues they can resolve independently. Refunds under a certain amount. Shipping delays. Wrong items. Any issue that has a defined answer.

2. Issues that need the founder's input first, once. Edge cases where you need to decide the policy together, then the VA owns it going forward.

3. Issues that always need the founder. Usually less than 5% of tickets. VIP clients, legal concerns, PR-sensitive situations.

Once those three buckets are defined, the VA runs the inbox independently. You check in via a daily summary — not individual tickets. You stop reacting to email and start leading the business.
