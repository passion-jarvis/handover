## 1. WHAT TO SAY — Full Call Script

### Opening
> "Hi, is this [Business Name]?"
> "Hi, my name is Shemily, I'm calling from Jarvis. Quick question for you — do you guys have a website right now?"

*(If they say no — perfect, keep going)*
*(If they say yes — "Got it, sorry to bother you!" and hang up)*

---

### The Hook
> "So the reason I'm calling is — I actually looked up your salon on Google, you have [X] reviews and a [4.X] star rating which is really impressive. But I noticed you don't have a website, so a lot of people searching for salons online can't find you."

> "We build landing pages for salons like yours in 48 hours. It's simple, professional, and it shows up when people Google you. A lot of our clients start getting new booking calls within the first week."

---

### The Offer
> "We do it for a one-time investment of $300, and we deliver it within 2 days. We handle everything — you just send us your logo if you have one, your services, and your hours. That's it."

---

### Handle Objections

**"I'm not interested"**
> "I totally get it. Can I ask — is it that you don't think a website would help, or just not the right time right now?"

**"I don't have time"**
> "That's actually the point — you don't have to do anything. We build it for you. You spend maybe 5 minutes sending us your info. We do the rest."

**"It's too expensive"**
> "I hear you. Let me ask you this — if one new client books from your website this month, does that cover the $300? For most salons it does in one visit. You're not paying for a website, you're paying for new clients finding you."

**"Let me think about it"**
> "Of course. What specifically do you want to think about? I'd rather answer any questions now so you're not sitting with something unclear."

**"Can I see an example first?"**
> "Yes — I can send you a sample right now while we're on the phone. What's the best number or email to send it to?"
*(Send them the Sitedrop demo link)*

---

### The Close
> "So should I get you set up? All I need is your email to send you the intake form, and we'll have your site live in 48 hours."

*(Get email → send intake form → done)*

---

### If They Want to Pay Later
> "We ask for payment upfront because we start building right away. It's $300 via card — takes 2 minutes. Want me to walk you through it now?"

## 2. HOW TO SELL

- **Lead with the compliment:** They have good reviews, real customers. A website just makes them findable.
- **Remove friction:** You do everything. They do nothing. 5 minutes of their time.
- **Use the ROI close:** One new client = covers the cost. Frame it as an investment, not a cost.
- **Build urgency:** "We only take a limited number of builds per week — we're almost full for this week."
- **Never fight the objection.** Say "I hear you" first. Then reframe.
- **Stay in control:** If they say let me think about it, ask what specifically. Don't let the call end without knowing the real objection.

---

## 3. HOW TO DELIVER

**Step 1 — Intake (same day as close)**
Send them this via email or WhatsApp:
> "To get started I just need:
> 1. Business name (exactly how you want it written)
> 2. Services you offer + rough prices
> 3. Hours of operation
> 4. Phone number for the site
> 5. Address
> 6. Logo (if you have one — doesn't matter if not)
> 7. Any photos of your salon (optional)"

**Step 2 — Build (Adithya builds it)**
- Hand intake info to Adithya
- She builds on Sitedrop.ai
- Done within 48 hours

**Step 3 — Deliver**
- Send client the live link
- "Here's your website — let me know what you think. We can make small tweaks within 48 hours."

**Step 4 — Upsell (optional)**
> "We also offer monthly hosting and updates for $50/month so you never have to worry about it. Want me to set that up?"
