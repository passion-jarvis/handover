## The Winning Formula

**Hook structure that converts qualified leads:**
```
Line 1: Revenue qualifier ("Doing $10k, $20k, $30k a month...")
Line 2: Pain reframe ("That's not a hustle problem. That's a systems problem.")
Line 3-4: Jarvis solution (automation angle)
Line 5: Social proof signal (Day 1 ready, pre-trained)
CTA: Low-friction ("10 seconds to see if you qualify")
```

**Creative angles in order of priority:**
1. **Automation differentiation** — VAs who build systems, not just take tasks
2. **Revenue qualifier** — speak to $10k+ owners, exclude others
3. **Speed to value** — Day 1 ready, $0 upfront, no hiring headache
4. **Founder cost of inaction** — still doing everything yourself at $X/month is losing money

## Production Specs by Format

### Meta Feed Video
- Ratio: 1:1 (square) primary, 4:5 portrait secondary
- Length: 15-45s (hook must land in first 3 seconds)
- Caption: Always include (80% watch muted)
- Format: MP4, H.264, max 4GB

### Meta Feed Static
- Ratio: 1:1 or 4:5
- Size: 1080x1080 or 1080x1350px
- Text on image: <20% of image area (Meta rule)
- Format: JPG or PNG

### Meta Lead Form
- Keep form short: Name, email, phone, revenue range, business type
- Revenue question must be first qualifier question
- Auto-disqualify $0-$5k (don't waste Shemily's time)

### YouTube Pre-Roll (when launching May)
- Ratio: 16:9 landscape
- Length: 30s (skippable after 5s — hook must work in 5s AND 30s)
- Format: MP4

### Google Responsive Search Ads
- Headlines (up to 15): 30 characters each
- Descriptions (up to 4): 90 characters each
- Always include revenue qualifier in at least 3 headlines
