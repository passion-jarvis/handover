## Goal-to-Budget Math

To hit 100 clients by June 30:
- Need 78 more clients in 13 weeks = **6 closes/week**
- At 30% close rate = 20 booked calls/week
- At 15% book rate from leads = 133 qualified leads/week
- At 40% quality rate from total leads = **~47 total leads/day** needed at peak
- At $20.75 CPL → **$975/day = ~$29,000/month** to hit 20 qualified leads/day via Meta alone

**Realistic math at $20.75 CPL:**
- 7 leads/day needed → $145/day → **~$4,350/month** at steady state
- That's a very achievable 2.4x from current $60/day spend
- Dec 30 deadline = 39 weeks — controlled, sustainable ramp

**Current baseline:** $60/day on Meta US = ~$1,800/month

**Ramp to Dec 30:**
- Apr-May: Confirm quality fix, scale to $100/day → ~$3,000/month
- Jun-Aug: Scale proven winners, launch Google → ~$4,500-6,000/month
- Sep-Dec: Full scale, launch YouTube → ~$7,000-9,000/month

**Note on scaling:** The 20% weekly rule applies per campaign. New campaigns (retargeting, Google) can launch independently — total spend can jump faster than 20% as long as each individual campaign scales correctly.

## Budget Pacing Rules

1. **Weekly check:** Is daily spend within 10% of target? If overspending, pause lowest performers.
2. **Scale trigger:** 2 consecutive weeks below $25 CPL (qualified) → increase Meta by 20%.
3. **Cut trigger:** Platform at 2x Meta CPL after 4 weeks → reallocate to Meta.
4. **Max weekly increase:** 20% per campaign. Never go from $100/day to $200/day in one move.
5. **Month-end review:** Reallocate based on actual CPL data. Don't stick to percentages if the math says otherwise.
