## Evaluation Checklist (run at every EVAL point)

**Green light (scale 20%):**
- [ ] CPL < $20 for past 2 weeks
- [ ] Quality rate > 35% (more than 1 in 3 leads is $10k+/month)
- [ ] Booked calls on pace (Shemily booking 7+/week)
- [ ] Close rate holding (George closing 30%+ of calls)

**Yellow (hold):**
- [ ] CPL $20-25
- [ ] Quality rate 25-35%
- [ ] Calls booking but close rate dropping

**Red (investigate before touching budget):**
- [ ] CPL > $25 for 2+ weeks
- [ ] Quality rate < 25%
- [ ] Booked calls dropping week over week
- [ ] Close rate below 20%
