## MAP — Massive Action Plan

### 🔴 PRIORITY 1: Fix the Lead Problem (April 21 — May 7)
The meeting made one thing clear: you're getting garbage leads from Meta and wasted money can't connect on calls.

**Actions:**
- [ ] Fix Meta lead form — add revenue qualifier BEFORE they submit (radio button: "My monthly revenue is: Under $5K / $5K-$10K / $10K+"). Anyone under $10K doesn't get submitted.
- [ ] Stop spending on phone cold call outreach — pickup rate is near zero per meeting feedback
- [ ] Build a "Mini Passion" VA workflow: your own VA follows up on every lead via email, not Passion herself — this demonstrates the product and converts better
- [ ] Create 3 specific case studies with before/after numbers (store rating 4.5 → 4.7, time saved, revenue impact) — this is the #1 thing blocking conversion at events and on calls

### 🔴 PRIORITY 2: LinkedIn Machine (April 21 — ongoing)
Free lead gen, zero cold call dependency. The meeting confirmed this works.

**Actions:**
- [ ] Assign Shemily or a VA to LinkedIn full-time: connect with 20 solopreneurs/co-founders per day (no message needed, just connect)
- [ ] Passion posts 3x/week on LinkedIn: case studies, "what a VA actually does", results posts — build trust passively
- [ ] After connecting, wait 3-5 days then send a soft message: "Hey [name], I help business owners at $10K+/month get their inbox and calendar back. Would you be open to a quick call to see if we're a fit?"
- [ ] Target ICP: solopreneurs + co-founders doing $10K+/month in e-commerce, restaurants, service businesses

### 🟡 PRIORITY 3: Networking That Actually Converts (May 1 — June 30)
The meeting was clear: showing up once = nothing. You need a system.

**Actions:**
- [ ] Pick 2 chambers to commit to for the quarter (research: Irvine Chamber, Newport Chamber, OC one that has business owners not employees)
- [ ] Show up every month, same ones — don't jump around
- [ ] Before each event: have VA pull attendee list, flag top 5-10 prospects, Passion researches them + reaches out 3 days early to set up a quick coffee at the event
- [ ] Don't sponsor unless you get a speaking slot. If you speak: 10-minute case study, assume audience has never heard of VA. Show visuals. Before/after. Real numbers.
- [ ] Don't do logo-only sponsorships. Period.

### 🟡 PRIORITY 4: Webinar Engine (May 15 — June 30)
Free webinars = warm leads + content machine. Even if 2 people show, that's 2 potential clients.

**Actions:**
- [ ] Build a 20-minute webinar: "How $10K+/month business owners reclaim 20 hours a week" — case studies, live demo of what VA does
- [ ] Run it twice a month on Zoom, free
- [ ] Record every session → chop into reels for IG + LinkedIn
- [ ] Promote via LinkedIn, email list, networking events (leave flyer on table)
- [ ] Goal: 5 signups per webinar by June

### 🟢 PRIORITY 5: Retention + Upsells (Ongoing — Ruth owns this)
You can't keep acquiring and leaking. Every churned client is -$1,600/month.

**Actions:**
- [ ] Ruth asks every client for a referral at 30-day and 90-day mark, every time
- [ ] Ruth identifies 3 upsell candidates per month (clients already happy → pitch second VA)
- [ ] Build a simple 1-page case study from your best client result — Ruth uses this in upsell convos
- [ ] Monthly NPS check-in with all clients (5 questions max, Google Form)
