# Case Studies — Raw Stories

## Story 1 — Construction
"We had a client doing about $3M a year in general contracting. Every Monday morning he'd spend 3 hours chasing subcontractors for updated insurance certificates and unpaid invoices. His VA now handles all of that — sends the reminders, follows up twice, flags anything overdue. He got those 3 hours back every single week. That's 150 hours a year he's now spending on site visits and closing new bids. His revenue went from $3M to $4.2M in 12 months."

## Story 2 — Real Estate
"One of our clients is a real estate agent doing about 30 transactions a year. Her biggest problem was speed — Zillow leads come in, and if you don't respond in 5 minutes, they're gone. She was losing deals just because she was showing a house when a new lead came in. Her VA now responds to every new lead within 2 minutes, qualifies them, and books a call. Her conversion rate on leads went up 40%. She closed 12 more deals last year without working more hours."

## Story 3 — Financial Advisor
"We worked with a financial advisor managing about $50M AUM. His problem wasn't clients — it was prep. Every client review meeting took him 2 hours to prepare: pull account summaries, review last meeting notes, update the CMA. His VA does all of that prep the day before. He walks into every meeting already ready. He went from 3 review meetings a week to 7. Same effort, more than double the output."

## Story 4 — Coaching / Consulting
"A business coach doing $600K a year came to us because she was drowning in DMs. She had 400 Instagram DMs unread, and she knew some of them were leads — she just had no time to sort through them. Her VA now manages her DMs daily: responds to general questions, flags real leads, books discovery calls. In the first month, her VA booked 11 calls she would have never gotten to. Three of them closed. That's $15K from messages that were just sitting there."

## Story 5 — D2C Brand
"We worked with a D2C skincare founder doing about $1.2M a year on Shopify. She was the CEO, the customer service rep, and the social media manager all at once. Every morning she'd wake up to 40-50 customer emails — where's my order, wrong item shipped, refund requests, subscription cancellations. By the time she got through them it was noon and she hadn't touched anything that actually grew the business. Her VA took over the entire customer service inbox: responds to every ticket within 2 hours, processes refunds and exchanges, handles subscription changes, and flags anything that actually needs the founder's decision. She went from 4 hours a day in her inbox to zero. She used that time to launch two new products. Revenue went from $1.2M to $1.8M in 8 months."
