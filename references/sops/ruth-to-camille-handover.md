### SOP 4: Client Check-In Cadence + Churn Prevention

**Check-in frequency:**

| Client Status | Frequency | Format |
|--------------|-----------|--------|
| New client (Days 1-30) | Weekly | Phone call, 15 min |
| Stable (Months 2-3) | Every 2 weeks | Call or WhatsApp |
| Long-term (Month 4+) | Monthly | Phone call, 30 min |
| Any active issue | Daily | Until resolved |

**Churn Warning Signals — act immediately on any of these:**
- Client response time noticeably slows
- Check-in gets rescheduled or skipped without explanation
- Client mentions budget, business slowdown, or "evaluating costs"
- Any complaint about the VA — treat as the tip of the iceberg
- Client asks how to pause or cancel

**Churn Save Protocol:**
1. Report to Passion the same day you spot a signal
2. Call the client within 24 hours — do not text
3. Script: "I want to make sure you're getting the value you expected. Is there anything I can do better?"
4. If it's a VA issue: present a resolution plan within 48 hours
5. Log the incident and resolution in the Client Tracker

## Referral Ask SOP (Inheriting from Ruth)

Kamille inherits the referral program. Daily targets:
- 2 referral asks per day
- 5 referral leads per week into GHL

**When to ask:**
- Onboarding complete (client is excited)
- Positive check-in (client expresses satisfaction)
- Problem resolved (client is relieved)
- Milestone hit (client reports a win)
- Upsell accepted (highest trust moment)

**Tracking:** Log every ask in the Referral Tracker regardless of outcome. "Asked Y/N" is the metric.
