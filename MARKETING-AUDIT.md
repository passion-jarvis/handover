## Executive Summary

Jarvis scores 60/100. The vetting story (1 in 200, 67-point assessment, 7 stages) and client results ($1.2M to $1.8M, $3M to $4.2M, 46x/62x ROI) are legitimately strong. No competitor at this price range can match that combination. The problem is the site is not converting what the traffic is worth.

The single biggest issue is CTA fragmentation: eight competing calls-to-action across the homepage with no hierarchy. "Hire an Automation VA," "Calculate What You're Losing," "Start my free pre-audit," "See how Jarvis works for you," "Book a Free 15-Min Call," "Book Your Free Match Call," "Meet Your Perfect VA, Guaranteed," and "Get This for My Business" are all live simultaneously. At $1,600/month, buyers need confidence and clarity. They are getting confusion instead.

The second biggest issue is no email capture. The entire conversion funnel ends at one outcome: book a Calendly call. Every visitor who does not book is permanently lost. With Meta ads paused, 42 warm leads sitting uncontacted, and LinkedIn organic just launching, revenue is evaporating daily.

The third issue is that Passion's face and story are not on the live site. The Why We Exist page is built but not published. At $1,600/month, founders buy the founder's judgment and accountability as much as they buy the service. A faceless company is harder to trust than a founder with a story.

Implementing the top three quick wins (collapse CTAs, add email capture, publish Why We Exist page) alongside reactivating the 42 warm leads could generate $4,800 to $9,600 in incremental MRR within 30 days at zero additional ad spend.
